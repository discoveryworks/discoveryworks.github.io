# Pizza Labs Privacy Policy

**Last updated: {{ site.time | date: "%B %d, %Y" }}**

This Privacy Policy describes how Discovery Works ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use Pizza Labs applications, including Pizza Pong, Pizzaroids, Tic Tac Pizza, and Pizza Ping (the "Apps").

## General Practices (All Apps)

### Information You Provide
- **Game Progress**: We may store your game scores and progress locally on your device to enhance your gaming experience.

### Information We Collect Automatically
- **Device Information**: We may collect information about your device, including device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
- **Usage Information**: We may collect information about how you use the Apps, such as game sessions and app interactions.

### How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve the Apps
- Understand how you use the Apps
- Provide customer support
- Ensure the security and integrity of the Apps

### Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage**: Game data is stored locally on your device
- **Security**: We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information
- **Retention**: We retain information only as long as necessary to provide our services

## App-Specific Privacy Information

### Pizzaroids

**Current Version:** 2.0.0+
**Uses Advertising:** Yes (Google AdMob)

#### Additional Information Collected by Pizzaroids

**Advertising Identifiers:**
When you use Pizzaroids, we may collect your device's advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS) if you grant permission through the App Tracking Transparency prompt. This identifier is used solely for showing you relevant advertisements.

#### How Pizzaroids Uses Information

In addition to the general practices above, Pizzaroids uses collected information to:
- Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
- Improve ad performance and relevance

#### Third-Party Services in Pizzaroids

**Google AdMob:**
Pizzaroids uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use information according to their privacy policy: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

When you grant tracking permission through the App Tracking Transparency prompt, AdMob uses your advertising identifier to show you more relevant ads.

#### Your Choices in Pizzaroids

**App Tracking Transparency:**
You can choose whether to allow tracking when prompted. You can change this choice anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

**Opting Out of Personalized Ads:**
You can reset your advertising identifier in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Reset Advertising Identifier.

#### Data Deletion for Pizzaroids

To delete your data:
1. Uninstall the Pizzaroids app
2. All local game data will be deleted from your device
3. To reset your advertising identifier, go to iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Reset Advertising Identifier
